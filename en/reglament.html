Tournament regulations

1. Registration and verification

01Registration will be closed an hour before the tournament starts. You need to fill an application form on the main page to register.
02Tournament starts on August 27th at 12:00 GMT.
03At the start moment the tournament lobby number and link for the WEB-clients will be published on the main page.
04To take part in the tournament you need to verify your application.
05To verify the application you just have to enter the tournament lobby with any available client before the games starts.
06The list of verified players will be published on the main page.
07Check if you application is verified!
08If you are not verified automatically in three minutes or your Tenhou nickname has been changed, please, tell about it to matteplaysmahjon@gmail.com
09Sending the application and entering to the tournament lobby at the day of the tournament the player confirms that he is familiar with the regulations and agree with all the terms.
10Please attend. Players who do not submit the application for registration will not be able to participate in the tournament, even if they participate in the verification process.
11The final list of players from all who will be formed in about 5 minutes after the tournament starts.
12If players count will not be four fold, the bots shall be added to the tournament. Their results will not affect the total scores.

2. While playing

01On the main page the seatings for all the four rounds will be published.
02To play you need just being in lobby before the game starts. Of course, if you have confirmed your application before.
03All the missing players will be highlighted in the table of seatings. If they are missing for 5 minutes from the start of the round, their participation in the tournament will be stopped, and they will be replaced by bots.
04After the game ends players can send the replay URL in provided form on the main page, but this is not necessary. After a while it will be linked to appropriate game in the table of results.
05For all the completed games the results (scores and places of all players) will be published along with a replay URL, if it was sent by someone.
06First round starts in about 12:05 GMT, right after seatings are prepared.
07Second round starts in 5 minutes after the last game of first round ends.
08Third round starts in 15 minutes after the last game of second round ends.
09Fourth round starts in 5 minutes after the last game of third round ends.
10Approximate ending time for the tournament: 16:30 GMT. But all of us know the game, it can be unpredictable long.

3. Game rules

01Game length — hanchan.
02Game end with one of the conditions:
02.1— at least one player goes below 0 game points;
02.2— wind changes on fourth South game round and at least one player has more than 30'000 game points;
02.3— dealer wins in fourth South game round and gets first place in current game.
03Kuitan (open tanyao) — allowed.
04Akadoras (red fives) — three, one in each suit.
05Uma and oka — no.
06Double ron — allowed, triple ron — draw.

4. Choosing the winners

01After each round all players receive tournament points in amount equal to difference of game points between the current player and the player who took the third place on the same table. So the players on the first and the second places get tournament points, player on the last place lose tournament points and player on the third place did not receive or lose anything.
02The more tournament points player have, the higher its position in the total table.
03Top three players of the tournament devides the prize fund of $75:
03.1— player on the first place gets $45;
03.2— player on the first place gets $22.5;
03.3— player on the first place gets $7.5.
04The process of receiving the prize will be discussed individually.
05All commissions are paid by winner.
06We will contact all the winners by provided contact details.
07If you win the prize but not left any contact details — tell about it to matteplaysmahjong@gmail.com
